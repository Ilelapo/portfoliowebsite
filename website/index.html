About Ilenia

A Short Story About Me

Ilenia is a Graphic and Product Designer. Her works are focused on Logotype, Brand identity, editorial design and graphic illustrations. She likes to experiment with different areas and techniques. She never find the perfect result in her projects but this helps her to improve more and more.

My studies are centered on Design on different declinations. I have an excellent command on graphic design and familiarity in Product Design (CAD software: Rhinoceros, Blender 3D, Keyshot). I also dedicate myself to the study and practical development of sites and projects using web technologies (Wordpress, Html5, Css3, Javascript). I constantly apply myself to keep up to date on the best tools and technologies so that I can best implement the ideas and projects that arise from my creativity.

Asas Fins

A new experience of living the sea.

Asas Fins is a project of a snorkeling fin adaptable to different sizes and shapes of the foot.
They take inspiration from Vibram's Furoshiki shoe model.
Thanks to the elastic and ergonomic bandage, they allow you to wear the fin with or without a rock shoe and are easily adaptable to different sizes and shapes of the feet.

How do they work?
1. Arrange the barefoot or with the rock shoe so that the end of the fingers touches the raised tip of the sole present on the blade.
2. Bring the first wing of the fin along the back of the foot going to girdle the whole ankle to then bring the end of the band back to the side of the foot where there will be velcro that will join the two extremities.
3. Carry out the same procedure as in step 2 but in the opposite direction.

Cantina
Kaltern

Label

Canyina Kaltern a well-known South Tyrolean winery, launches the 4th edition of kunst.stück. The theme of the competition is "Kissed by the sun" and is dedicated to the Merlot Riserva which, in the hot 2018 vintage, saw its grapes really ripen to perfection.

on the label you can find the element "nature" in the mountains and in the colors from which it is represented. The mountains become, fading, the face of a woman (taken from the famous painting by Gustav Klimt) who appears serene, protected and even more beautiful because she is kissed by a hot sun. It is precisely in this warm sun, which kisses the woman's face with its light, bringing out its beauty, that the goodness and uniqueness of this wine can be sought. Unique because it is the result of the magnificence of nature combined with the beauty and simplicity of a kiss.
